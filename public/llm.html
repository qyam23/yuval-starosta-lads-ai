Starosta Industrial

Starosta Industrial is an industrial engineering practice focused on practical manufacturing environments. Yuval Starosta Labs is the supporting industrial data and intelligence layer used where operational visibility, anomaly detection, and structured manufacturing data are needed.

The website describes Starosta Industrial, Yuval Starosta Labs, ER Labs, extrusion systems, automation and control systems, industrial intelligence, factory work, and process engineering.

Core scope includes co-rotating twin screw extruder work, extrusion troubleshooting, PLC and HMI structure, industrial automation, control systems, factory engineering, MES-adjacent data visibility, and reporting environments such as Power BI where appropriate.
